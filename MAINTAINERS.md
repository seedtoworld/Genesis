
This document defines the maintainers of Project Genesis and their responsibilities.

Maintainers are responsible for guiding the development of Genesis, reviewing contributions, and ensuring that the project follows its design principles and architecture.

## Current Maintainers

AN32 – Project Founder

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing pull requests
- Ensuring code follows DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md
- Guiding architectural decisions
- Managing releases
- Maintaining project documentation
- Moderating community discussions
## Decision Making

Major decisions should be discussed openly in issues or discussions.

The project founder has final decision authority when consensus cannot be reached.

## Becoming a Maintainer

A contributor may become a maintainer if they:
- Have made significant contributions to the project
- Demonstrate understanding of the architecture
- Consistently review and help other contributors

New maintainers are appointed by existing maintainers.

## Maintainer Inactivity

Maintainers who become inactive for extended periods may be moved to emeritus status.

## Maintainer Conduct

Maintainers must follow the standards defined in CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md.
